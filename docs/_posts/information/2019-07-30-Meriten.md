# Meriten

this is a collection of things that Digital Media students have _achieved_ as part of their activities in our program at HfK. _Meriten_ is german for _merits_.

please contribute! if you ever find your work to be published, rewared, mentioned. recoginzed etcetera please send a brief note to [Prof. Dennis P Paul](mailto:dennis.paul@hfk-bremen.de) or submit your meriten via [this form](http://dm-hb.de/meriten-add).
